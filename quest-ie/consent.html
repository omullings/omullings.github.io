Data Protection and Freedom of Information Notice

The HSE will treat all personal information and data you provide as part of this application as confidential and store it securely. When the HSE receives your application and supporting documentation, it will make a computer record. This record will contain the relevant personal information you have supplied and will be used and retained by the HSE, solely for the purposes of assessing your eligibility (and that of your spouse, or partner if relevant and named dependants) under the Medical Card scheme. Anonymized data may be disclosed to the other bodies for the purposes of providing health service. Any disclosure will be in accordance with the laws relating to the proper treatment of data.

The HSE will not disclose (share) to the other people or organizations the personal information you have given unless permission has been given by the person to whom the information relates or the HSE is required to do so by law.
